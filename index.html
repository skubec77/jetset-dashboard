How Scoring Works
Every reformer is evaluated across 7 inspection sections, ~24 individual checkpoints
HEALTHY
90 – 100%
All critical safety items pass. Minor cosmetic or non-safety issues acceptable.
WATCH
75 – 89%
No safety fails, but multiple non-critical items need attention. Schedule maintenance.
CRITICAL
< 75%
Below threshold OR any safety-critical item failed. Take reformer out of service.
⚑ SAFETY-CRITICAL SECTIONS
A fail on any item in Springs or Straps auto-flags the reformer as Critical regardless of overall score. These are the load-bearing components most likely to cause injury if compromised.
Score formula: (Passed checkpoints ÷ Applicable checkpoints) × 100. N/A items are excluded from the denominator. Location score is the average of all on-site reformer scores.
